FAQ

How do I include AdSense on my site?
This template already includes your requested AdSense meta tag. To show ads you will also need to add AdSense script tags and ad placements — follow Google's official setup flow and policy. Don't forget to verify your site in your AdSense dashboard.
Is this accessible?
Yes — semantic HTML, well-ordered headings, keyboard-friendly controls, and visible focus states are included. Use an automated a11y checker and manual testing for full compliance.
